Self Care R Us Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

This site is a place to write about activities that are easy to do to take care of yourself. In this modern era of internet and social media, it is easy to put thoughts about ourselves to the side. This website will have ideas and suggestions on how anyone can be happier and live a better life!

Target Audience

My audience is targeted more towards women who are constantly multitasking and are busy. The demographic for these women is from young married to older, so about 18-40 years old. These women are generally located in either the USA or Canada, or other English speaking countries. My target audience has graudated from high school and completed some college. The average of hours my target audience works is at least 20 hours a week, part-time workers and full-time mothers.
