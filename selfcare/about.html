Let me introduce myself

My name is Abby. I am a senior in college and I, like so many other students get stressed, stay up too late, and do it all over again the next day.

Life doesn't get less busy, only more. I don't have it all figured out, but I do have some tips. That's why I made this website, as a place to not only share what I know with others, but to remind myself of the princples of what caring for yourself can do in your life!

Why SelfCare

Well, why not? Why not take time for yourself? You should.

I think self care is something that would, if put in to practice, help the lives of so many people. It's something I think about often.

It's not something that has to cost a lot of money or take a lot of time. It should be something you make time for. That's all it costs, is your own time. Plus, it's so worth it.
