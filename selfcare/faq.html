General

What does selfcare mean?

The whole concept of self care is about taking time for yourself, to hit pause, to reset your priorities, all that good stuff. Really, it is meant to boost your productivity. Sometimes, our world around us throws us into a space where we can't really be positive. But I have found that it is essential to make time, and space, for positivity.

Why does self care matter?

Self care helps you access the best parts of your personality. No one can be a giver, or focusing on others, all the time. You need time for you. So, make a date with yourself! You won't be disappointed.

How can I tell if I am doing enough?

Everyone is different, so what my right amount of self care may be will likely not be the same for you. But we can learn from one another. I like to ask myself, "In the last 3 days, what have I done for myself?" If the answer is, "Not much," I know I've got some work to do.
